Introduction

     Creative Brains Academy Inc., formerly known as Cinco’s Learning Center, is located at 69 Tobleron Street, Espiritu, Balite, Rodriguez, Rizal. Established in 2018, the institution initially provided educational programs for nursery, kindergarten, and preparatory levels. By 2019, it expanded to include grade one, followed by the addition of grade two in 2020. Over the years, the academy has continued to grow, incorporating higher grade levels, and now provides education up to grade six.


     The academy prides itself on fostering a nurturing and supportive environment that encourages young minds to thrive. They teach their students with a focus on good and positive teaching practices, emphasizing the development of both academic excellence and strong moral values. By blending engaging and interactive learning methods with a commitment to individual growth, Creative Brains Academy ensures that its students are not only well-prepared academically but also equipped with the essential skills and attitudes to succeed in life. Their educators strive to inspire curiosity, creativity, and a love for learning, making the school a place where students can truly excel and feel valued.



Why Us?


     At Creative Brains Academy, we are committed to shaping bright futures through quality education, character-building, and a supportive learning environment. What sets us apart is our child-centered approach, dedicated teachers, and our focus on early literacy, academic growth, and values formation. We create a place where every child feels safe, encouraged, and empowered to reach their full potential—academically, emotionally, and socially.



School Events


     We believe that learning goes beyond the four walls of a classroom. That’s why Creative Brains Academy hosts a variety of school events and activities designed to support holistic development. These include academic fairs, reading and literacy programs, arts and talent showcases, values education activities, nutrition month celebrations, and foundation day festivities. Through these events, students develop essential life skills such as teamwork, confidence, and creativity, all while having fun and discovering new interests. Because of our excellent learning environment and meaningful activities, our students grow not just in knowledge but in experience.


     With us, your child will surely learn how to read during preschool. Our dedicated educators utilize innovative and effective teaching strategies tailored to young learners. We focus on fostering early literacy skills through engaging activities, interactive lessons, and individualized attention. At Creative Brains Academy, we believe that developing strong reading skills at an early age sets the stage for a lifetime of academic success and a love for learning.
